Mechanical Walker creature model & animation

During my studies' fifth module, we had to model a mechanical walking creature. It was based on a tutorial. However, to make the model suitable for games, we had to find ways to cut down on geometry to make it able to be run in real-time without lagging the computer. We also had to make a seamless walking cycle.

In the end, I was quite happy with the result. It took tens of hours to build, including all the modelling, texturing, rigging and animating. Everything was done by me from scratch!

You can view the final animation through this link.

Keep in mind that it is subject to YouTube's (not-great-looking) video compression, so it may not look as good as it would in a game.

The teachers based the tutorial on a video from Polyfjord, which can be seen here.
